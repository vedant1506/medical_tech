Common Mental Health Issues in Youths

Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety disorders involve excessive fear or worry. They include generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and social anxiety disorder.

Depression

Depression causes persistent feelings of sadness and loss of interest in activities. It can affect one's daily life and overall well-being.

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder causes extreme mood swings, including emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression), which can disrupt daily activities.

Obsessive compulsive disorder(OCD)

it is a condition where people have repeated, unwanted thoughts (obsessions) and feel the need to do certain actions or rituals (compulsions) to try to make the thoughts go away. These actions can take up a lot of time and make daily life harder, even though the person may know the rituals don't really help.
